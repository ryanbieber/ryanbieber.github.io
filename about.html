Background!

You can view my resume here.

I am Ryan Bieber, a data scientist based in Albert Lea, MN, with a strong background in AI, data science, and machine learning engineering. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics, followed by a Master of Science in Applied Economics from the University of North Dakota. During my academic journey, I had the privilege of interning at the Mayo Clinic, where I worked on advanced imaging techniques, as well as at an economic consulting firm, where I focused on data analysis for class action lawsuits.

Currently, I serve as a Senior Data Scientist at C.H. Robinson, where I have developed innovative solutions such as a Gen AI pipeline for automating customer bid generation and an integrated time series forecasting platform. I have also created a news-gathering application using NLP to provide the sales team with a competitive edge. My work includes deploying sophisticated forecasting models and building defect monitoring dashboards, which have significantly enhanced operational efficiency and delivered valuable insights for the business.

Prior to my role at C.H. Robinson, I worked as a Financial Data Scientist at IBM, where I utilized NLP techniques to improve efficiency across finance departments and developed applications that saved considerable time. I also enjoyed mentoring junior data scientists and received several awards for my contributions while there. With over five years of experience in designing and deploying applications within cloud environments, I am dedicated to leveraging my skills to help businesses optimize their operations through data-driven solutions.
